What is a “Server” and a “Space”?

A server powers your space — it’s the private (or public) 3D room where people connect, move, and talk. The server handles:

  • 🧠 Multiplayer connections (movement inside your space)
  • 🎙️ Proximity voice chat (hear people when you’re close, fade when you walk away)
  • 🔒 Access mode — Public Free Space or Private Pro/Max Space

Think of it as your digital room: Free Spaces are public (anyone with the link can join). Pro/Max Spaces are private (you control who can access).
