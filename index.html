FAQ

Do I need an account to try ALYSSIUN?

No. You can enter LITE worlds in your browser without an account. Some HD/VR features may require sign-in later.

What engines are supported?

Unity, Unreal, and any engine that outputs a performant WebGL (LITE) or desktop build (HD).

How do creators earn 80%?

Publish assets, apps, or worlds to our marketplace. Sales revenue is split 80/20 in favor of creators.

Is VR supported?

Yes — VR is available on select HD builds and will expand over time.
